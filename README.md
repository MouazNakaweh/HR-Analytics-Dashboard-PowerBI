## ğŸ“Š Dashboard Features  

The HR Analytics Dashboard consists of the following pages:

1. **Executive Summary**  
   - High-level overview of the organization's HR metrics, including total employees, attrition rate, department headcounts, and key KPIs.  

2. **Attrition Analysis**  
   - Detailed insights into employee turnover, trends over time, attrition by department, role, age, tenure, and risk factors for leaving.  

3. **Compensation & Performance**  
   - Analysis of employee salaries, performance scores, bonus eligibility, and correlation between compensation and performance.  

4. **Work-Life & Satisfaction**  
   - Visualizations related to employee satisfaction, work-life balance, engagement surveys, and potential areas of improvement.  

5. **Employee Profile / Tenure**  
   - Individual employee profiles, tenure analysis, and retention metrics to monitor long-term workforce trends.  

6. **Employee Info**  
   - Comprehensive dataset view including personal details, department, role, joining date, education, and other HR-related information.
